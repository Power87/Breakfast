# Breakfast

  <h1> What are we making? </h1>
  <h2> by Hanah Billups </h2>
  
  <p> I like to make this breakfast during cold winter mornings <br/> when I have a long day ahead of me. <br/> This hearty breakfast will give you plenty of energy to attack <br/> the cold and sleepinees.
  </p>
  
  <h2> Ingredients </h2>
  
  <ul> <li> 1 cup Steel Cut Oats </li>
    <li> 1/3 cup of 2% milk </li>
    <li> 1 tspn. honey </li>
    <li> 1 tbsp. brown sugar </li>
    <li> 1 banana </li>
    <li> 1/4 cup of raisins </li>
  </ul>
  
  <h2> What to do </h2>
  
  <ol> <li> Start by filling a small pot with the milk </li>
    <li> Set the stove to medium high and wait for the milk to come to a boil </li>
    <li> Next pour the oats into the milk and turn the stove down to medium </li>
    <li> While the oats are cooking cut the banana into whatever size slices you wish</li>
    <li> Once the oats are thick and the milk has been absorbed turn off the stove</li>
    <li> Immediately pour the honey into the oatmeal </li>
    <li> Pour the oatmeal into a bowl </li>
    <li> Pour the raisings on top of the oatmeal and surround with banana slices </li>
    <li> Lastly sprinkle the brown sugar on top and Enjoy! </li>
  </ol>
